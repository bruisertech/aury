Practice Area 02

Corporate Law & Business

Comprehensive legal protection for companies, investors and entrepreneurs in Colombia. From corporate formation to economic criminal defense. Includes the specialty of Financial Habeas Data for credit history defense before Datacrédito and TransUnion.

The viability and growth of any company in the Colombian economic environment require preventive and strategic legal shielding. At Aury Díaz & Asociados, we operate as allies to the corporate sector, structuring efficient corporate vehicles, preventing tax contingencies, and protecting intangible assets. Furthermore, our firm stands out for its forceful defense of Financial Habeas Data, ensuring that violations of the credit history of entrepreneurs and individuals are corrected with the full weight of the law.

Commercial & Corporate Law

Accompaniment in the life cycle of the company, from its conception to its eventual liquidation, including processes of reorganization or expansion.

  • Formation of SAS, S.A., Ltd. — Law 1258/2008
  • Commercial Code — Decree 410/1971
  • Mergers, spin-offs and acquisitions
  • Business insolvency — Law 1116/2006
Legal Framework

Commercial Code · Law 1258/2008 (SAS) · Law 1116/2006 (Insolvency)

Tax & Customs Law

Optimization and defense against tax obligations before the DIAN, ensuring regulatory compliance and reduction of contingencies.

  • Tax Statute — Decree 624/1989
  • Tax reform — Law 2277/2022
  • Defense before DIAN: sanctions and appeals
  • Simple regime and electronic invoicing

Intellectual Property & LegalTech

Real Estate & Property Law

Advice to co-ownerships, construction companies and administrators under the regime of Law 675/2001.

Financial Habeas Data

Technical defense of your credit history against credit bureaus (Datacrédito, TransUnion). We verify validity and periods of permanence to restore your financial life.

  • Habeas Data Financiero — Ley 1581/2012 · Ley 1266/2008
  • Right of petition and appeals before the SIC

Legal Shielding for Complex Business Decisions

Business decisions always carry legal risks. From tax structuring according to the Tax Statute and recent reforms, to defense before the Superintendence of Industry and Commerce for Habeas Data (Laws 1581 of 2012 and 1266 of 2008), our firm offers solutions that protect your company's operational continuity and reputation.
