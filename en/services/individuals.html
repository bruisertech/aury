Practice Area 03

Persons & Family Law

Comprehensive legal counsel for the most transcendent decisions in your personal, professional and family life. Also includes legal representation for Colombians abroad and personal insolvency services.

Decisions involving the family core, personal assets, and employment stability are undoubtedly the most determining factors in anyone's life. Our approach in this practice area combines the technical firmness required for litigation with the empathy and discretion necessary for family and economic insolvency matters. Through the Non-Merchant Personal Insolvency Law, we provide effective legal tools for liability restructuring and restoring financial peace of mind.

Labor & Social Security Law

Defense of workers' rights and advice to employers on the correct application of Colombian labor regulations.

  • CST — Decree 2663/1950: employment contracts
  • Termination with and without cause, severance
  • Workplace harassment — Law 1010/2006
  • Union, maternity and reinforced employment protections
  • Pensions, ARL, EPS and social security
Legal Framework

CST Decreto 2663/1950 · Ley 100/1993 (SS)

Family Law & Inheritance

Management of family conflicts with the required sensitivity and legal rigor. Inheritances, divorces, and protection of minors.

  • Custody, alimony, divorce, adoption
  • Common-law union — Law 54/1990
  • Domestic violence — Law 294/1996
  • Estates: intestate and testamentary

Personal Insolvency

Legal solutions for non-merchant natural persons in a situation of over-indebtedness, allowing a second financial opportunity.

  • Personal insolvency — Arts. 531-576 CGP
  • Debt negotiation and asset liquidation

Legal Clarity for the Future of Your Family and Assets

Navigating the complexities of the Substantive Labor Code, the Civil Code regarding family matters, or the General Code of Procedure (Arts. 531 to 576) for insolvency processes requires specialized accompaniment. We design clear routes to liquidate liabilities, ensure fair labor compensation, and resolve family conflicts while minimizing emotional and financial impact.
