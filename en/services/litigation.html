Practice Area 01

Litigation & Conflict Resolution

Strategic defense at all levels of Colombian courts. Led directly by Aury Fernán Díaz Alarcón, this is the firm's core practice area. Top-level criminal specialization backed by 10+ years of active litigation in the Colombian accusatory system.

Our litigation practice is based on rigorous dogmatic analysis and relentless evidentiary strategy. At Aury Díaz & Asociados, we understand that facing criminal, disciplinary, or state liability proceedings compromises not only assets but also freedom and reputation. Therefore, under the direct leadership of a Master and Specialist in Criminal Law, we deploy a proactive defense that seeks to neutralize contingencies from preliminary stages, relying on the highest jurisprudential standards of the Colombian High Courts.

Criminal Defense & Accusatory System

Under the framework of Law 906 of 2004, we guarantee a comprehensive technical defense, assuming the representation of our clients with strict adherence to due process and fundamental guarantees in each procedural stage.

  • Technical defense from investigation to oral trial
  • Pre-agreements and negotiations — Arts. 348-354 CPP
  • Opportunity principle — Art. 324 CPP
  • Habeas corpus — Law 1095/2006
  • Precautionary measures and substitution
  • Economic crimes: embezzlement, bribery, procedural fraud
Legal Framework

1991 Political Constitution · Ley 906 de 2004 (CPP) · Ley 599 de 2000 (CP)

Public & Disciplinary Law

We protect the interests of public servants and state contractors against investigations and statements of objections. We act diligently to disprove administrative and disciplinary liability.

  • Defense before the Procuraduría — Law 1952/2019
  • State Liability — CPACA Law 1437/2011
  • Administrative nullities
  • Fiscal responsibility processes (Comptroller)

Civil Litigation & Liability

Attention to declarative, executive and contractual and extra-contractual civil liability processes under the provisions of the General Code of Procedure (Law 1564 of 2012).

Transitional Justice & Human Rights

Specialized technical representation before the Special Jurisdiction for Peace (JEP) in accordance with Law 1957 of 2019, ensuring access to the benefits of transitional justice and guaranteeing due process in voluntary or mandatory appearances.

Your Defense Leaves No Room for Improvisation

Sanctioning criminal and disciplinary law in Colombia demands a profound understanding of the accusatory system (Law 906 of 2004) and the General Disciplinary Code (Law 1952 of 2019). Do not delegate your freedom or professional career to standardized defenses. We have the courtroom experience to ensure every constitutional guarantee is respected and enforced.
