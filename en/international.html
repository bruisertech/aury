Specialized Service

Legal Representation in Colombia — No Travel Required

If you are in the United States, United Kingdom or another country but need to act legally in Colombia, we assume your full representation through a duly apostilled or consulate-authenticated power of attorney. No travel required.

Service Scope

  • Investments & real estate (purchase, fraud, management)
  • Family & estate (successions, divorces, liquidations)
  • Business & companies (formation, shareholder representation)
  • DIAN obligations (tax returns, administrative defense)
  • Criminal and disciplinary processes involving non-residents
1
Virtual Consultation: Free and confidential initial video call to evaluate your case from any country.
2
Power of Attorney: We guide you step by step to grant power before a local notary and apostille the document.
3
Full Representation: We act on your behalf before all Colombian authorities with periodic reporting.
4
Follow-up: Progress reports in Spanish and English. Communication via WhatsApp, email and video call.
